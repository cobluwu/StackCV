🚀 StackCV

StackCV is an AI-powered resume analysis and optimization tool I built to explore how modern hiring systems (ATS + recruiters) evaluate candidates.

Instead of treating resumes as static documents, this project focuses on making them data-driven and improvable.

---

🌐 Live Demo

👉 https://stack-cv.vercel.app/

---

🧩 What it does

- 📊 Analyzes your resume like an ATS
- 🔍 Compares it against job descriptions
- ✍️ Rewrites content using AI
- 📤 Exports an ATS-friendly version
- 🎯 Helps you prepare for interviews

---

✨ Features

📊 ATS Match Scoring

Breaks down how well your resume aligns with a specific role, including:

- Role relevance
- Strength of evidence
- Seniority alignment

---

🔍 Keyword Gap Analysis

Highlights missing skills and keywords that impact shortlisting.

---

✍️ One-Click AI Resume Rewrite

Generates a cleaner, stronger version of your resume with:

- Impact-focused bullet points
- Better phrasing
- ATS-safe formatting

---

📤 Resume Export

Download a version that is optimized for parsing and readability in ATS systems.

---

📈 FutureFit Insights

Gives a structured view of where your profile stands and what to improve next.

---

🎯 Interview Intelligence

Generates role-specific interview questions with structured answer guidance.

---

⚙️ Tech Stack

- React + TypeScript
- TailwindCSS
- AI APIs (LLM-based processing)

---

🎯 Why I built this

Most students (including me) don’t know:

- why their resume gets rejected
- what “ATS optimization” actually means
- how to improve beyond generic advice

StackCV is my attempt to make that process:

«clearer, measurable, and actionable»
